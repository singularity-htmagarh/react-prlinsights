Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical engagement take?

Most projects range from 3-8 months depending on scope. Analytics audits can be completed in 4-6 weeks, while full implementations typically take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What data do you need to get started?

We typically need access to your advertising platform data (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.), web analytics, CRM/sales data, and any existing attribution or analytics tools. We'll work with your IT team to ensure secure data access.

Do you work with small to medium businesses?

Our sweet spot is mid-market to enterprise companies with monthly marketing budgets of $100K+. This ensures there's sufficient data volume and business impact to justify advanced analytics approaches.

How do you ensure data security and compliance?

We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification, use end-to-end encryption, and are fully GDPR and CCPA compliant. All data processing happens in secure, audited environments with strict access controls.

What makes your approach different?

We apply rigorous statistical methods from academia and clinical research to marketing data. Instead of just showing correlations, we prove causal relationships using experimental design and advanced econometric techniques.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, we offer various ongoing support options including monthly optimization reviews, quarterly strategy sessions, and dedicated analytics team partnerships for continuous improvement and adaptation.
